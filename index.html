Dental Services in Bhopal

Every dental need,
expertly handled.

Care 'N' Cure Dental Clinic offers a full range of dental treatments in Bhopal's Arera Colony — from routine check-ups to advanced restorative procedures, all in a calm, hygienic, and patient-first environment.

01
General Dentistry
Comprehensive oral check-ups, professional scaling and cleaning, tooth-coloured fillings, and preventive care — the foundation of a lifetime of healthy teeth.
02
Root Canal Treatment
Gentle, single or multi-visit RCT that eliminates infection, relieves pain, and preserves your natural tooth — avoiding unnecessary extraction.
03
Crowns & Bridges
High-quality ceramic and metal-fused crowns and fixed bridges that restore the strength, appearance, and function of damaged or missing teeth.
04
Teeth Whitening
Clinically supervised, professional-grade bleaching that removes deep staining and discolouration — delivering a noticeably brighter smile, safely and effectively.
05
Tooth Extraction
Simple and surgical extractions performed under effective local anaesthesia — including wisdom tooth removal — with clear post-care guidance for quick recovery.
06
Dentures & Prosthetics
Complete and partial removable dentures custom-fabricated for a secure fit, natural aesthetics, and restored chewing ability — giving you your confidence back.
07
Pediatric Dentistry
Specialised, fear-free dental care for children — from first milk teeth to teenage years. We make every visit positive so your child grows up comfortable with dental health.
08
Orthodontics & Braces
Metal and ceramic braces for children, teens, and adults — correcting misaligned teeth and improving bite for a straighter smile and better long-term oral health.

Meet Your Dentist in Bhopal

Dr. Sanjeev
Bajpai, BDS

"My goal has always been simple — treat every patient the way I'd want my own family treated. With honesty, skill, and genuine care."

Dr. Sanjeev Bajpai holds a Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) and has been practising dentistry in Bhopal since 1990 — over 34 years of full-time clinical experience. Based at 11th Stop, LIG Colony in Arera Colony, his clinic has served thousands of patients ranging from young children on their very first dental visit to senior citizens requiring complex restorations.

Patients consistently praise his methodical diagnosis, unhurried consultations, and his ability to perform procedures — including root canals and extractions — with remarkably little discomfort. Whether you're visiting for a routine scale-and-polish or a full dental rehabilitation, Dr. Bajpai brings the same calm, thorough attention to every case.
